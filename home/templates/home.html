About Us

We specialize in creating magical transformations with makeup and mehendi for every occasion.

Welcome to "Makeup and Mehandi Art", where beauty meets tradition! We are a passionate team of professional makeup artists and mehndi (henna) designers dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty and adding an artistic touch to your special moments. Whether you’re looking for a flawless makeup look or intricate mehndi designs, we provide personalized services tailored to your style and occasion.

Our Expertise

Makeup Services: From everyday elegance to bridal glam, our makeup services are crafted to make you look and feel your best. We specialize in bridal makeup, party makeup, HD makeup, and airbrush techniques, ensuring long-lasting and picture-perfect results.
Mehndi Art: Our mehndi designs blend tradition and creativity, offering intricate patterns for weddings, festivals, and celebrations. From classic bridal mehndi to trendy contemporary styles, our designs are sure to leave a lasting impression.

Why Choose Us?

Personalized Approach: Every client is unique, and we work closely with you to create looks that reflect your personality and preferences.
High-Quality Products: We use only premium-quality makeup products and natural henna to ensure safety and flawless results.
Attention to Detail: Whether it’s a perfect winged eyeliner or a symmetrical mehndi motif, we pride ourselves on precision and excellence.
On-Time Service: We value your time and ensure timely delivery of our services to keep your day stress-free.
At "Makeup and mehandi" Art, we believe that beauty is an art, and every occasion is a canvas. Let us be a part of your journey to create unforgettable memories with our makeup and mehndi artistry. Contact us today to book your session and experience the magic of beauty and tradition!
